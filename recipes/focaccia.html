Focaccia

Description:

Focaccia is a flat oven-baked Italian bread, often seasoned with olive oil and herbs.

  • Ingredients:
    • Flour
    • Water
    • Yeast
    • Olive oil
    • Salt
    • Rosemary or other herbs
  • Steps
    1. Mix flour, water, yeast, and salt to form a dough.
    2. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
    3. Let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size.
    4. Shape the dough into a flat rectangle on a baking sheet.
    5. Dimple the surface with your fingers and drizzle with olive oil.
    6. Sprinkle with salt and herbs.
    7. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
