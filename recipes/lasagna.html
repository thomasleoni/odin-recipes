Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and bolognese sauce

  • Ingredients:
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Ricotta cheese
    • Shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Ground beef or sausage
    • Marinara sauce
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Eggs
    • Garlic
    • Onion
    • Olive oil
  • Steps
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
    3. Sauté garlic and onion in olive oil, add ground meat and cook until browned.
    4. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
    5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and some Parmesan cheese.
    6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
    7. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
    8. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
